About Me

I’m interested in problems that are just structured enough to be solvable and just unpredictable enough to stay interesting, especially in machine learning and related areas. I spend a fair amount of time working with models that don’t always cooperate, trying to get them to do something useful anyway. Somewhere between debugging, experimenting, and convincing myself that the next run will finally converge, I’ve developed a preference for challenges that reward persistence more than elegance. It’s less of a hobby and more an ongoing negotiation with complexity, and I tend to stick with it until it gives in.
